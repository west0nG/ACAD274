Glossary of Key Terms

FG%
Field Goal Percentage. The ratio of field goals made to field goals attempted, expressed as a decimal (e.g., .443 means 44.3%).
3P%
Three-Point Field Goal Percentage. The ratio of three-point field goals made to three-point attempts.
FT%
Free Throw Percentage. The ratio of free throws made to free throws attempted.
REB
Rebounds. The total number of times a player or team retrieves the ball after a missed field goal or free throw.
AST
Assists. The number of times a player passes the ball to a teammate in a way that leads to a score.
TO
Turnovers. The number of times a team or player loses possession of the ball to the opposing team.
@
Indicates an away game. For example, "@NYK" means the game was played at the New York Knicks' home arena.
